URGENT - PROJECT IN PROGRESS Construction Phase | Funding Needed

🏥 Construction of a Maternity Block with Operating Theatre

Nanton District, Northern Region – Ghana | Comprehensive Emergency Obstetric and Newborn Care (CEmONC)

📋 The Crisis

Maternal mortality remains a critical public health concern in rural districts. The Nanton District, with an estimated population of 58,700 residents, currently has no hospital. The Health Centre lacks capacity to manage obstetric emergencies requiring surgical intervention. Recent district health data shows a disturbing rise in preventable maternal deaths.

🏥 Project Scope

Construction of a modern Maternity Block with fully functional operating theatre at the Nanton Health Centre, enabling Comprehensive Emergency Obstetric and Newborn Care (CEmONC) services including emergency surgical interventions.

Labour Ward
Lying-In Ward (10-15 beds)
Ultrasound Room
Consulting Room
Family Planning Unit
Operating Theatre
Recovery & Sterilization
Pharmacy & Records

🎯 Expected Outcomes

  • ✓ Reduction in maternal and neonatal mortality
  • ✓ Increased access to safe delivery services
  • ✓ Timely management of obstetric complications
  • ✓ Reduced emergency referrals outside the district
  • ✓ Upgrade Nanton Health Centre to Polyclinic status

Prepared by: Dr. Sheba Kunfah
Nanton District Director of Health Services
